Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026  |  Omenda Pi Pays Global

🔒 Your Privacy Matters. This Privacy Policy explains how Omenda Pi Pays Global ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application on the Pi Network platform.

1 Information We Collect

When you use Omenda Pi Pays, we may collect the following information through the Pi SDK with your explicit consent:

  • Pi Network Username — Your public Pi Network username, used to identify you within our marketplace.
  • Pi User ID (UID) — A unique identifier provided by Pi Network for account linking and transaction processing.
  • Access Token — A temporary authentication token used to verify your identity with Pi Network's servers. This token is not stored permanently.
  • Payment Data — Transaction amounts, memos, and payment identifiers for orders processed through Pi.createPayment.

2 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or process:

  • Your Pi wallet private keys or passphrase
  • Your real name, email, phone number, or physical address (unless you voluntarily provide it in a service form)
  • Your precise device location or GPS coordinates
  • Contacts, photos, or files from your device
  • Any biometric data

3 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for the following purposes:

  • Authentication — To verify your identity and allow you to access marketplace features.
  • Transaction Processing — To create, approve, and complete Pi payments for goods and services.
  • User Experience — To display your username in the app and personalize your session.
  • Order Management — To track your purchases across our services (hotels, property, bill payments).
  • Customer Support — To assist you with order issues or disputes.

4 Data Storage & Security

We take the protection of your data seriously:

  • Session Storage — Authentication data (username, UID) is stored in your browser's session storage and is automatically cleared when you close the browser.
  • Server-Side Verification — Access tokens are verified server-side with Pi Network's API (api.minepi.com) and are not stored in our database.
  • No Third-Party Sharing — We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • Encryption — All communication with our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.

5 Pi Network SDK Permissions

Our app may request the following Pi Browser permissions, each requiring your explicit consent:

  • Camera — Only when you choose to upload a photo through a form (e.g., product listing). We do not access your camera in the background.
  • Share Dialog — To allow you to share products or referral links through Pi's native share feature.
  • Clipboard (copyText) — To copy reference numbers or transaction IDs to your clipboard when you request it.

You can deny any permission request and continue using the app with reduced functionality.

6 Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser session storage to maintain your login state during a browsing session. We do not use tracking cookies, third-party analytics, or advertising pixels.

7 Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with:

  • Pi Network Platform — For authentication and payment processing. Pi Network's own Privacy Policy applies to data processed by Pi Network.
  • Pi Ad Network — If you interact with ads, Pi's ad network may collect data as per Pi Network's policies.

We do not integrate with any other third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

8 Children's Privacy

Omenda Pi Pays is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

9 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your data from our systems.
  • Withdraw Consent — Revoke your Pi Network data sharing consent at any time through the Pi Browser app settings.
  • Data Portability — Request your data in a machine-readable format.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
