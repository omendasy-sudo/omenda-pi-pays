Security and Responsible Disclosure

We continuously improve application security and welcome responsible vulnerability reports.

Account and Wallet Safety

  • Never share your wallet seed phrase or private keys.
  • Verify usernames and listings before sending payment.
  • Report suspicious activity immediately through the Scam Report page.

Report a Security Issue

Email us at abuse@omendapipaysglobel.online with:

  • A clear description of the issue.
  • Steps to reproduce.
  • Proof-of-concept details (if available).
  • Potential impact assessment.

We also publish security contact metadata at /.well-known/security.txt.

Scope

Testing must be lawful, non-destructive, and respectful of user privacy. Do not attempt denial-of-service, data exfiltration, or attacks on third-party infrastructure.
